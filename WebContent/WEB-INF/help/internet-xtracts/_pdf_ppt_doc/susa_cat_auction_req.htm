Catalogue Auction Guidelines
Bidding may begin when the items become available at SoldUSA.com or when a catalogue is received. Your bidder number must accompany all bids entered. Please confirm all telephone bids with a written copy of the amount bid and the item number, delivered via US mail or facsimile.

Telephone Bidding Schedule
Telephone bids may be submitted Monday through Friday, 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Eastern time. These hours will be extended to 11:00 p.m. Eastern time on the final days of the auction. Please refer to page one of the catalogue or the catalogue home page for the dates applicable to the current auction. Any customer attempting to deny other customers telephone access may be disconnected at the discretion of SoldUSA, Inc. operators. The time each bid is entered is electronically recorded.
Facsimile Bidding Schedule
Bids faxed on the closing days of an auction must be received by 9:00 p.m. Eastern time. Bids received after the deadline will not be considered. Please refer to page one of the catalogue or the catalogue home page for the dates applicable to the current auction.
Registering a Bid
Each item in an auction has an estimated value range as provided by SoldUSA. The minimum opening bid for each item is 50% of the lowest estimated value for that item. If an estimated value is not provided for an item, the minimum opening bid is $1.00. Bids are only accepted in whole dollar increments and partial dollar bids are rounded up to the next whole dollar. All bids registered will must be denominated in US Dollars. If a registered bid is tied, the first bid registered will be accepted as the winning bid. Occasionally the catalogue description number does not match the photograph number. To ensure that your bid is properly entered, always bid based on the catalogue description number, not the photograph number. Winning bids must be in an amount greater than the reserve established by the Seller, provided that such a reserve is set.
Bid Advancement
Bids are advanced in minimum increments of 5% of the last bid registered. Fractional amounts are raised to the next whole dollar. Advancements made in less than 5% increments will not be accepted. Received spread bids that exceed the current bid or another bidder's spread will win the item even if the 5% rule is not met if, in the opinion of the auctioneer, the raise was made in good faith and exceeds the other's spread by a sufficient amount as not to cause ill will.
Optional Automatic Increase of Spread Bids
You can elect to have the Service automatically place bids for you up to a selected maximum bid amount. By inserting an amount in the maximum bid block, you indicate to the Service that you wish to continuously bid in the required minimum increments until the bidding reaches the amount inserted in the maximum bid block. The maximum bid will not be published or displayed to any other users. For example, the current bid on an item is $50.00 and you wish to bid on this item up to a maximum bid amount of $100.00. If activated, the Service will automatically increase your bid incrementally by 5% to outbid the highest bid at any given point. This process will continue until either: a) your bid becomes the winning bid, or b) the bidding surpasses your maximum bid amount of $100.00
Special Shipping Arrangements
Large, heavy, or fragile items may require special shipping arrangements. Each customer is responsible for special shipping arrangements and the associated additional charges.
International Sales
Each customer is required to make necessary arrangements for freight forwarding, customs clearance and payment of duties that may be levied. SoldUSA, Inc. will include a copy of the invoice with all international shipments.
Purchase Summary
Telephone, mail and facsimile customers may request a summary of purchases made on the second day following the auction close. Requests, including the return facsimile number or e-mail address, must be submitted via telephone or fax no later than the day following the close of the auction. Internet customers will receive an electronic invoice immediately following the close of the auction.
Forms of Payment
SoldUSA, Inc. accepts credit cards (Visa, Mastercard and Discover only), certified checks, money orders and wire transfers. A credit card number and expiration date are required to be on file for all customers. All cards are verified prior to the auction; however, charges are applied only if a purchase is made. If you elect not to use a credit card to settle purchases, you may open an account with SoldUSA, Inc.
Settlement of Accounts
For Catalogue Auctions only, all accounts must be settled within 25 days of the auction close. If an account has not been settled by the 25th day, the customer account will be charged to the credit card number on file. Insufficient funds will result in the loss of purchases, and the item will be sold to the next highest bidder, provided that such bid is greater than the required minimum bid and/or reserve, if applicable.
Returned Checks
Any member who makes deposits to his/her account with a check that is subsequently returned will be charged a fee of $30.00
Lost or Stolen Checks
If a participant notifies SoldUSA, Inc. that a check has not been received, SoldUSA, Inc. will promptly request a stop payment of the check. Any bank service charges incurred by SoldUSA, Inc. will be the responsibility of the participant.
Taxes
You are responsible for paying all applicable taxes (including, but not limited to, sales taxes, regardless of whether these taxes are included in any payment to SoldUSA.) Residents of North Carolina are subject to sales tax charges. All other customers are responsible for directly submitting any sales tax required by the laws of their state of residence to the appropriate taxing authority. You agree to hold SoldUSA, Inc. harmless for any sales tax liability that arises outside the state of North Carolina. North Carolina businesses must provide SoldUSA, Inc. with a valid wholesale/resale license issued by the state of North Carolina.


Shipping
Winning customers are responsible for all actual shipping, handling and insurance fees. These amounts will be added to your invoice according to the chart below. You may be billed for additional shipping and handling depending upon actual cost. A minimum charge of $7.50 applies to any purchase regardless of size or weight. Allow 3-4 weeks delivery from date we receive payment. All shipping and handling is done in-house. The customer may request any carrier for any shipment in writing before the close of an auction. We strongly suggest that you use FedEx 2 day service for important of easily damaged items. If no special request in made, we will use UPS. We will insure each shipment for full value. Shipment dates for your purchases will be in order of payments received. Please do not contact our office for ship dates until 30 days following the close of an auction. The customer agrees to inform SoldUSA, Inc. of any damage occurring in shipment. SoldUSA, Inc. will settle all claims leaving our premises with the carrier. DO NOT RETURN any damaged item until authorized by SoldUSA. SHIPPING AND HANDLING FEES ARE NOT REFUNDABLE.
